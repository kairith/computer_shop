    Apple M4 chip on Ipad

    The Apple M4 chip is a system-on-a-chip (SoC) designed by Apple for their latest iPad Pro devices. It is the successor to the M3 chip and is built using second-generation 3-nanometer technology .

  • 2024

    iOS 17.5.1

    iOS 17.5.1 is an update focused on fixing a bug where deleted photos reappeared in the Photos app. It doesn't include new features, and some users have reported new issues after installing it. If you were affected by the photo bug, update to 17.5.1. Otherwise, you might wait to see if Apple fixes the new bugs reported with 17.5.1.
